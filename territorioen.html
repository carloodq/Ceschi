The firm

CESCHI-BRUGNOLI is a family business that was born in 2009 from the deep passion for the territory and for sustainable viticultore that, since we were born, has enveloped us in all its elegance and its sweetness, but has also taught us the waiting and hard work.

Valpolicella valley

Our company is spread over almost 6 hectares distributed in various plots in the Valpolicella Classica between the municipalities of Sant'Ambrogio di Valpolicella and Fumane, between the city of Verona and Lake Garda.

Vineyards

The vineyards are cultivated with guyot with an average of about 4500 vines per hectare and are all located in the hills between 240 and 590 mt o.l.s.

Respect

We have always been conducting an environmentally friendly agriculture without the use of chemical weeding and pay particular attention to preserving the biodiversity of the territory.
